--- layout: page title: "DH Pedagogy" permalink: /dh-pedagogy/ nav: true --- The lab offers expertise in digital humanities (DH) and experimental scholarship pedagogies, including at the undergraduate, graduate, and post-graduate levels. We offer consultation, syllabus review, co-teaching, and other forms of support for developing digital pedagogy and implementing it in the classroom, and our leadership and participation in the [external DH community](/critical-dh) allows us to inform and be informed by the latest DH pedagogy research and practice. We frequently publish on critical digital pedagogies, including in peer-reviewed journals such as [The Journal of Interactive Technology and Pedagogy](https://cuny.manifoldapp.org/read/a-way-in-digital-pedagogy-training-with-speculative-low-tech-workshops/section/d26fab6f-c473-489a-9516-a6e6f0c747a7); in edited volumes including The Digital Futures of Graduate Study in the Humanities and What We Teach When We Teach DH: Digital Humanities in the Classroom; and in other public formats such as [Digital Pedagogy in the Humanities: Concepts, Models, and Experiments](https://digitalpedagogy.hcommons.org/) and #DLFTeach Toolkit: Lesson Plans for Digital Library Instruction.
