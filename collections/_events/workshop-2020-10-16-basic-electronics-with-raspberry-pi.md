You will need to provide the following equipment: 
- Raspberry Pi, 
  - power adapter
  - SD card
- HDMI monitor, 
- HDMI cable (Raspberry Pi 4 uses a micro-HDMI port), 
- USB keyboard, 
- USB mouse, 
- breadboard, 
- jumper wires, 
- 100 Ohm resisters, 
- red, green, and yellow LEDs (or any color you can get)
