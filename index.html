About Us

The Scholars’ Lab, part of the UVA Library’s DH Center, is an interdisciplinary lab for the critical exploration of technology and its interventions on research, scholarship, and pedagogy. Our staff are informed by digital humanities, computer and information science, spatial technologies, maker, and cultural heritage approaches. While much of our work focuses on humanistic questions, we are open to all and welcome collaborations from across campus

Our Spaces

The Lab includes co-working areas, an event space, a VR immersion space, and data processing workstations. It is open whenever Shannon Library is open, but appointments are encouraged if you want to meet a particular staff member or ensure access to specific equipment.

The Makerspace is a place for tinkering and experimentation with technologies like circuitry, 3D printing, and laser cutting. Open to everyone, we specialize in applications and research questions in the humanities and arts.
